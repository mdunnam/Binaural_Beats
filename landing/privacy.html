Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

BlinQ, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates Liminal (https://www.theliminal.app). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address. This is stored securely in Supabase, our authentication and database provider.

Usage data: We collect basic usage information such as feature interactions, session length, and preferences. This helps us improve the app. This data is stored in Supabase and associated with your account.

Payment data: Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full credit card number, CVV, or other sensitive payment details — those go directly to Stripe's secure systems. We only receive a confirmation of your subscription status and billing cycle.

2. Data Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)

The following data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Journal entries — personal notes and reflections you write in the app
  • Custom presets — your saved audio configurations
  • OpenAI API key — if you choose to use the AI meditation prompt feature, you provide your own OpenAI key, which is stored locally in your browser only

We have no access to this data. It exists only on your device.

3. OpenAI Integration

Liminal includes an optional feature that generates personalized meditation prompts using the OpenAI API. This feature is opt-in and requires you to provide your own OpenAI API key.

When you use this feature: your input text (meditation prompts or session context) is sent directly to OpenAI's API using your own API key. This data is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy. We do not store, log, or retain any prompts or responses generated by OpenAI on our servers.

If you do not use this feature or do not provide an OpenAI API key, no data is sent to OpenAI.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Send you important service-related notifications (e.g., billing confirmation, account changes)
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

5. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Period.

6. Third-Party Service Providers

We work with the following trusted third parties to operate the Service:

  • Supabase — authentication and data storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (Privacy Policy)
  • OpenAI — optional AI content generation, only when you use this feature with your own API key (Privacy Policy)

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

7. Cookies and Session Storage

Liminal does not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We use only the session cookie required by Supabase to keep you authenticated while you use the app. This cookie is necessary for the Service to function and is deleted when your session ends or you log out.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account and usage data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer (e.g., billing records).

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data. You can initiate this from your account settings or by contacting us.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Opt out of any non-essential communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at legal@theliminal.app.

10. Children's Privacy

Liminal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice within the app. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
